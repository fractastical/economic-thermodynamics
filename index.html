How to read this

Gini and Top 1% show concentration. Entropy shows dispersion of money. Velocity approximates transaction volume divided by agent money.

The collision preset intentionally couples visual radius to wealth, like the original demo. The clean yard-sale preset decouples trade matching from the display.

The virtual-world preset is deliberately simple: creators produce goods, consumers buy through a marketplace, land boosts visibility, and the platform collects fees/tier.
